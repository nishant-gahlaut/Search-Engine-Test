b'\nChef has given you a sequence A[1], A[2], ..., A[N] composed of N nonnegative integer numbers. Thus, we have obtained N*(N-1)/2 numbers. Your task is to find K minimal numbers among them.\n\nInput\nThe first line of the input contains two space separated integers N and K.
